# Hostfunc Roadmap

## Near-term (0-2 months)

- Launch hardening and self-host setup flow.
- CLI stability for deploy, run, logs, and secrets.
- Trigger reliability for cron and inbound email.
- Documentation site and operational runbooks.

## Mid-term (2-6 months)

- Plan enforcement and billing lifecycle completion.
- More executor backends (Lambda/Fly/Deno) behind shared contract.
- Stronger multi-tenant audit trails and policy controls.
- Production-ready observability and alert integrations.

## Long-term

- Marketplace and reusable function templates.
- Team and enterprise admin controls.
- Advanced scheduling and event routing features.
